                                                                                          iforce2d Mod Tom 3 years ago

                                                                                          The b2Dot function returns the 'dot product' of two vectors. Dot product is a very common thing when dealing with vectors and you can find information about it all over the internet. It's usually used to find out how similar the direction of the two vectors is.

                                                                                          As a brief summary:
                                                                                          Suppose you have two vectors, each of length 1. Their dot product will be 1 if they are pointing in exactly the same direction, and -1 if they are pointing in exactly opposite directions. If they are perpendicular to each other, the dot product will be zero. For angles in between, the dot product will be the cosine of the angle between them (and negative if they are pointing away from each other).
